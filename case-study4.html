Kavorka - Beauty Rebranding

Project Description

This project was a **cosmetic rebranding** for **Kavorka**, a beauty brand focused on elegance, confidence, and modern aesthetics. As part of the rebranding, we designed **eye-catching posters, engaging social media ads, and a compelling video campaign** to showcase the brand’s new identity. The visuals were crafted to resonate with beauty enthusiasts, emphasizing luxury, sophistication, and empowerment.
